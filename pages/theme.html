Theme Groups.

The Ensuing Tomorrow

What is happening today, that we are so doubtful about the Earth's survival? What kinds of human influences / natural phenomena are causing such dynamic changes in the earth's way of functioning? What is being done today that will ruin our tomorrow? What can be done today to make tomorrow better? Is it sustainable and ethical to exploit all the scarce resources and leave none for the future? We will find the answers to all these questions from a humanitarian, an artist and a scientist's point of view. This theme takes care of the future, and how can today's actions affect the well-being of tomorrow. Ecocentric and Technocentric approaches shall be applied here in order to draw out good solutions for the problems that will be identified during the course of the theme group discussions.
